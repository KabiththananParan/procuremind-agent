Demonstrate how AI agents can safely perform procurement workflows on behalf of verified employees using Terminal 3's trust infrastructure.

* AI-powered procurement

# Opening

## Introduction

Hello, we are presenting ProcureMind, an autonomous AI procurement platform powered by Terminal 3 Agent Auth.

Organizations spend significant time managing procurement requests, approvals, and compliance workflows.

ProcureMind transforms this process by combining AI agents with verifiable identity and authorization infrastructure.

Instead of filling out procurement forms manually, employees simply describe their needs in natural language while AI agents analyze requests, assess risk, recommend vendors, and route approvals.

Terminal 3 acts as the trust layer that verifies identities, approvals, permissions, and audit events.

# Step 10: Future Autonomous Procurement

## Screen

Delegation Concept

---

## Narration

One of the most powerful capabilities enabled by Terminal 3 is delegated authority.

Employees can grant limited purchasing authority to AI agents.

For example:

Allow software purchases under $500 for the next 30 days.

Once verified, the AI agent can autonomously evaluate vendors, assess risk, and execute procurement actions within approved limits.
